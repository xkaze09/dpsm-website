APPLIED MATHEMATICS

Course Credits: 2 Units

Prerequisites: Math 198.1

Math 198.2 - Special Problem II

Course Description

Conduct of research; writing and presentation of research results

Course Learning Outcomes

After completion of the course, the student should be able to:

  1. Apply appropriate research method to investigate a research problem.
  2. Produce a research output detailing solution(s) to a research problem.
  3. Evaluate a research output.
Course Outline

UNIT 1. Conduct of a Research Project

  1. Model Formulation
  2. Computational Experiment
  3. Results and Analysis

UNIT 2. Research Project Output

  1. Research Project Write-up
  2. Defense of a Research Project Output
