The Bachelor of Science in Statistics program of the University of the Philippines Visayas in Miagao, Iloilo was first implemented in June 2000. It is a four-year course that provides students with a sound understanding of the underlying theories and applications of Statistics. Currently, it prides itself on the fact that it is the only Statistics program being offered in Western Visayas.

The BS Statistics program adheres to the University of the Philippines admission policy for incoming freshmen at the bachelor's level. The program is one of the non-quota courses offered in the University of the Philippines Visayas. Although BS Statistics is one of the non-quota courses, (i.e. there's no limit in the number of accepted freshman students into the course), the program accepts 30-35 incoming freshmen students, on the average, every academic year.

The graduates of the B.S. in Statistics program should be able to:

  • demonstrate mastery of the theories and methods in Statistics
  • prepare for graduate studies, career in teaching, government and in various industries
  • apply statistical tools in analyzing data to extract information about national issues and interests especially in the field of fisheries and marine sciences
  • offer solutions and insights based on empirical knowledge in making decisions pertaining to certain social issues
  • effectively communicate results of statistical analyses for different stakeholders
  • implement studies with impact on local and national developments
  • work effectively and independently in multi-disciplinary and multi-cultural environments
  • demonstrate professional, social, and ethical responsibility in his career
