OUR MISSION

A center of excellence in mathematical and computational sciences in the Visayas.

OUR VISION

  • Offer graduate and undergraduate degree programs responsive and contributing to the regional, national and global needs.
  • Conduct theoretical and applied researches with concentration in the fields of mathematical and computational sciences.
  • Organize specialized training activities that will improve competencies and skills of stakeholders.
  • Produce globally competitive stakeholders.

More About UPV Division of Physical Sciences and Mathematics

Division of Physical Sciences and Mathematics (DPSM) of the University of the Philippines Visayas is composed of faculty members coming from the Computer Science, Statistics, and Physics clusters.

Currently, the division offers the three (3) degree programs:

  • B.S. in Computer Science,
  • B.S in Applied Mathematics,
  • and B.S. in Statistics.
