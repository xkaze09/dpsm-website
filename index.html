WELCOME TO THE DIVISION OF PHYSICAL SCIENCES AND MATHEMATICS!

DPSM is home to the dedicated mathematics, statistics, computer science, and physics faculty members and students of the College of Arts and Sciences. It is an academic unit that comprises four interrelated disciplines and offers undergraduate degree programs to students interested in pursuing careers in applied mathematics, computer science, and statistics. Our faculty’s diverse expertise provides up-to-date and relevant training that can broaden as well as deepen the students’ knowledge and understanding of their chosen disciplines, sharpen their computational and analytical skills and competencies, and enrich their research capability.
So, if any of these four disciplines is your cup of tea, join and   with us!
—Dr. Arnel L. Tampos, DPSM Chairman
