WELCOME TO THE DIVISION OF PHYSICAL SCIENCES AND MATHEMATICS!

We take pride in offering 3 strong academic programs: Applied Mathematics, Statistics, and Computer Science, supported by dedicated mentorship and access to well-equipped laboratories and learning spaces that is focused on outcome-based learning.

The Division of Physical Sciences and Mathematics (DPSM) is a community of passionate, capable students, faculty, and support staff sharing a common interest in Mathematics, Statistics, Computer Sciences, and Physics. More than just a place to study, DPSM is where curiosity meets purpose, anchored in the UP values of Honor, Excellence, and Service.

If you’re curious, driven, and ready to make a difference, DPSM is the place to grow. Let’s learn, lead, and serve together.

—Assistant Professor Kent Christian A. Castor, DPSM Chairperson
